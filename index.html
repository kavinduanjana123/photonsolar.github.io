We Are
Photon Solar Solutions (Pvt) Ltd

Photon Solar Solutions Pvt Ltd is an emerging solar PV system installation company that is passionate about promoting sustainable and renewable energy solutions As a newly registered business, we are driven by a fresh perspective and a strong commitment to delivering high quality solar installations for residential, commercial, and industrial clients

Vision

"To illuminate a brighter future powered by the sun, where renewable energy is accessible to all, empowering communities with sustainable solutions that revolutionize the way we harness and utilize solar power

Mission

“At Photon Solar Solutions Pvt Ltd, we're dedicated to delivering reliable, efficient, and affordable solar PV installations. Our mission is to empower clients with sustainable solutions, reducing carbon footprint and fostering a greener future

Our Services

One Stop For Solar Power Problems

At Photon Solar Solutions Pvt Ltd, we offer comprehensive solar PV system installation services, tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients.

Site Assessment

We conduct detailed site assessments to determine the feasibility of solar installations, considering factors such as sunlight exposure, roof conditions, and energy requirements

Custom Design

Our skilled engineers design customized solar PV systems that are optimized for energy generation and seamlessly integrated into the existing infrastructure

Installation

Our trained technicians handle the installation process with precision and expertise, ensuring safe and efficient system setup

Maintenance

We provide regular maintenance and monitoring services to ensure optimal system performance, maximizing energy production and longevity
